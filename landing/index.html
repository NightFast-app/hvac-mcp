Install — 30 seconds

Pick your client. Requires uv (curl -LsSf https://astral.sh/uv/install.sh | sh). No account needed for the free tier.

1. Open your config: ~/Library/Application Support/Claude/claude_desktop_config.json (macOS) or %APPDATA%\Claude\claude_desktop_config.json (Windows).

2. Paste this in (create the file if needed):

{
  "mcpServers": {
    "hvac": {
      "command": "uvx",
      "args": ["--from", "git+https://github.com/NightFast-app/hvac-mcp", "hvac-mcp"]
    }
  }
}

3. Restart Claude Desktop. Click the 🔌 icon in chat — you'll see seven hvac_* tools.

One command in your terminal:

claude mcp add hvac -- uvx --from git+https://github.com/NightFast-app/hvac-mcp hvac-mcp

Verify: claude mcp list

FAQ

The real questions, not the ones I wish you'd ask.

Is this a subscription trap?

No. Cancellation is one click in Stripe's customer portal — no call, no email, no retention hell. The moment you cancel, your license deactivates on the next billing cycle. 14-day full refund, no questions. I hate subscription traps too.

Does this replace my license or my engineer?

No — and anyone who tells you their software does is lying. Outputs are informational only. Every code citation includes a "verify with your AHJ" line. Every sizing result tells you what it didn't consider. This is a reference that runs fast on your phone — not a substitute for your ticket, your license, or an engineer when the job needs one.

What data do you collect?

Stripe holds your payment info — I don't see your card. The server stores your Stripe customer ID and a random license key, and that's it. No emails, no job data, no phone numbers, no location. Every tool call on the free tier runs 100% offline from bundled JSON — no network. Source is on GitHub, grep it yourself.

I've never installed anything with uvx. How hard is this?

It's one command in Terminal: curl -LsSf https://astral.sh/uv/install.sh | sh. Takes 15 seconds. Then you paste a 10-line config block into one file, restart your chatbot, and you're done. If that sounds intimidating, buy a hosted tier — there's nothing to install on your phone, just paste a URL into the Claude app's connector settings.

Does this work offline in the truck?

The free tools do — they're bundled reference data, no network required. Claude itself needs internet to think. Realistic middle ground: download the data once, phone has LTE on most calls, you're fine.

What if the data's wrong?

Open an issue on GitHub with the correction + a source (manufacturer PDF, IRC section, whatever). I fix it, redeploy, everyone gets it. Your correction goes into tasks/lessons.md so it doesn't regress.

Why not just use ChatGPT or Claude directly?

LLMs are good at understanding messy field descriptions; they're unreliable at calc math and code citations. MCP puts the calc + citation behind a tool call — so the chatbot never invents a saturation pressure or an IRC section number. Same chatbot, honest answers.

I'm a 2-tech shop. Do I need Pro?

No, Starter is fine. Pro adds FL permit lookup + priority support — useful if you're doing 10+ permits a month or you need me to pick up the phone fast. Start on Starter, upgrade if you hit the ceiling.

Lifetime — is the limit real?

Yes. 50 customers, one-time $399. I'd rather have 50 early believers than juice a subscription number. When it's gone, it's gone. After that, the tiers are monthly only.
